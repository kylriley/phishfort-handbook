# Phone-Related Attacks

## Smishing (SMS Phishing)

### Evidence Required

✅ **Sender Number** (preferable)
✅ **Receiver Number**
✅ **Date, Time, and Timezone** of the SMS
✅ **Text or Screenshot** of the SMS
✅ **Brief Description** of the attack (if possible)

## Phone Scams

### Evidence Required

✅ **Caller Number**
✅ **Receiver Number**
✅ **Screenshot** of the phone call with date and time
✅ **Timezone**
✅ **Brief Description** of the call/attack

## Best Practices

### ✅ Do This
- Include both numbers involved.
- Specify timezone clearly.
- Provide screenshots with timestamps.
- Describe what was said/requested.
- Explain the impersonation or scam.

### ❌ Don't Do This
- Don't omit timezone information.
- Don't provide just phone numbers without context.
- Don't skip description of the scam.
