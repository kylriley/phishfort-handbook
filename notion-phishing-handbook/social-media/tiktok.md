## Types of Violations

### Infringing Posts (Actionable)
- Posts using your brand assets without permission.
- Posts claiming false affiliation.
- Posts with copyrighted content.
- Posts impersonating your customer service.

### Profile Name Only (NOT Actionable)
TikTok does NOT take down profiles based solely on:
- Profile name containing your brand.
- Bio mentioning your brand (without false claims in posts).
- Profile picture similarity.
